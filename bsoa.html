WELCOME AMIGOS

At Santo Tomas College of Agriculture, Science, and Technology (STCAST), our Bachelor of Science in Office Administration (BSOA) program is dedicated to shaping competent, skilled, and value-driven professionals ready to excel in business, industry, and government service.

We strive to equip our students with:

- A solid foundation in administrative, medical, and legal office operations through comprehensive education.
- Hands-on experience connecting academic knowledge to real-world office functions.
- Specialized training for immediate employability in various office settings.
- Motivation to pursue continuous professional growth.

Our program aims to produce graduates who:

- Possess the technical skills, knowledge, and ethical values to excel professionally.
- Embrace self-discipline as a cornerstone of personal and societal development.
- Are prepared for success and growth in their chosen career paths.

Join us in building a future of skilled, disciplined, and purpose-driven professionals!
